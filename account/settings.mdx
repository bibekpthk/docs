---
title: "Preferences"
description: "Control display preferences and payout connection visibility."
---

<Steps>
1. Open the top‑right menu (hamburger or avatar) and choose <b>Account Settings</b>.
2. Or open directly: `https://journi.me/user/account-setting`.
3. Toggle visibility for supporter and follower counts if available.
4. If Stripe is supported in your country, connect or manage your payout account.
5. Save settings.
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" emoji="ℹ️">
Stripe only shows if it’s available in your country or you’ve already connected an account.
</Callout>
