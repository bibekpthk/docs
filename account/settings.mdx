---
title: "Preferences"
description: "Control display preferences and payout connection visibility."
---

<Steps>
1. Open Dashboard → Account Settings.
2. Toggle visibility for supporter and follower counts if available.
3. If Stripe Connect is supported in your country, connect or manage your payout account.
4. Save settings.
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" emoji="ℹ️">
Stripe Connect only shows if it’s available in your country or you’ve already connected an account.
</Callout>
