This guide walks you through everything you need to use Chiya Pasal confidently. From opening your page, to sharing your posts, to receiving support from your community, all the way to closing your account if you ever choose to.

Chiya Pasal is a digital cafe for creators. A place to share what you are working on and invite people who care about your journey to stand behind it. A cup of chiya is how support is expressed. Simple, familiar, and real.

### This manual covers
- Public profile pages
- Posting photos, videos, and notes
- One-time support and memberships
- Managing payouts and account settings
- Safety, privacy, and troubleshooting
- Common questions and how to get help

## About Chiya Pasal

- Website: https://chiyapasal.me

### What it is
A platform where creators share their process and supporters can buy them a cup of chiya or join as members to support the journey over time.

### Who it is for
- Creators who want to build a direct connection with their audience.
- Supporters who want to back the work, not just watch it.
