---
title: "Browse Creators"
description: "Discover creators and explore their work."
---

<Steps>
1. From the homepage, use the explore/search interface to find creators.
2. Filter or sort (where available) to locate profiles by interest.
3. Click a creator to open their public profile page at `/@username`.
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" emoji="⚡">
Public pages render even when you’re signed out. Some content may require membership to view.
</Callout>
