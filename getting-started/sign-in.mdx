<Steps>
1. Visit https://journi.me
2. Click Login.
3. Or open the sign-in page directly: https://journi.me/user/sign-in
4. Enter your email or username, and your password.
5. Complete the captcha if shown.
6. If Two-factor Authentication (2FA) is enabled, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
7. Select Sign In to continue to your dashboard.
</Steps>
