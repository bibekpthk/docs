---
title: "Verify Account"
description: "Email, phone, and two-factor verification."
---

After sign up, you may need to verify your email and optionally your phone.

<Steps>
1. Open the verification page at `/user/authorization` (auto-redirect after login if pending).
2. Click “Resend Code” if you didn’t receive the code.
3. Enter the code to verify your email (and phone if requested).
4. Optionally set up 2FA (Time-based one-time password) for extra security.
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⏱️">
Verification codes expire. If the code times out, resend and try again.
</Callout>

### KYC verification
Some countries require KYC for payouts. If your country is in the required list, you’ll be prompted in the dashboard to submit documents under KYC.
