---

<Steps>
1. Visit https://chiyapasal.me and select <Button href="https://chiyapasal.me" newTab>Get Started</Button>.
2. Choose "Create chiya pasal".
3. Enter:
   - A username (this becomes your public link: `https://chiyapasal.me/{username}`)
   - Your email
   - A secure password
4. Complete the captcha and submit.
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" emoji="ðŸ’¡">
Choose a username that is easy to say, remember, and search. If you already have a creator name, use that.
</Callout>

<Callout type="note" emoji="ðŸ’³">
Once your account is created, fill in your profile and payout details. This is required to receive payments.
</Callout>

## Sign Up with Google

<Callout type="success" emoji="ðŸ”—">
If available, you can sign up using your Google account from the same page. You will still need to verify your email and complete your profile before receiving support or payouts.
</Callout>
