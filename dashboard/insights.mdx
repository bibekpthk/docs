title: "Insights"
description: "Track earnings, tips, and trends over time."
---

Insights helps you understand how your support grows over time.

### What you’ll see
- Earnings: total earned in the selected period.
- Breakdown: one‑time tips vs membership earnings.
- Tips: number of tips sent over time.

### Filter & explore
<Steps>
1. Choose a date range (Today, Last 7, Last 30, Custom).
2. Hover charts to see exact values and daily totals.
3. Use breakdowns to compare one‑time vs membership trends.
</Steps>
