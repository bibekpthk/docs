---
title: "Inbox"
description: "Read fan messages and see recent supports."
---

The Inbox shows the latest activity in a familiar email‑style list. You’ll see messages supporters leave with their tips or memberships, along with amounts and timestamps.

<Steps>
1. Open your dashboard home.
2. Scroll to Inbox to read new messages.
3. Open an item to view the full message and details.
4. Mark items as read, dismiss, or load more.
</Steps>
