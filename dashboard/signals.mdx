### Getting started
- Connect your YouTube channel and return to Signals to see the "View my first insight" button on the Signals page.
- Click "View my first insight" to surface the recommendation, evidence, and actions that you can take right away.
- Each insight lands in the **Latest signal** block, which keeps the current recommendation and workspace actions front and center.

### Cadence & freshness
- **Manual refresh:** tap refresh once every 24 hours when you want a new look at the data.
- **Auto refresh:** Signals updates automatically after new uploads and during weekly reviews, delivering a new recommendation roughly 48 hours after each fresh upload.
- **Expiry:** insights expire after seven days, keeping only the most current next steps visible.
